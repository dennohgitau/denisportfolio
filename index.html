My AWS expertise includes architecting scalable cloud solutions, implementing security best practices, and optimizing infrastructure for performance and cost efficiency. My experience extends to colocation environments (iColo), where I manage physical infrastructure, implement redundancy strategies, and maintain optimal performance across distributed systems.

My server management skills encompass both Linux and Windows environments, including system administration, performance tuning, security hardening, and automated provisioning. I manage complex server infrastructures, implement monitoring and alerting systems, and ensure optimal resource utilization across development, staging, and production environments.

My technical expertise spans Python, Django, JavaScript, Angular, React, and Node.js, enabling me to create robust, scalable web applications that handle high traffic and deliver exceptional user experiences. I collaborate closely with clients to understand their business needs and deliver efficient, scalable, and user-friendly solutions that solve real-world problems. My approach combines technical excellence with creative problem-solving to bring innovative ideas to life.
